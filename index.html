What PKC Coin is

Two systems, one token

PKC Coin exists to do two specific jobs. It isn't a general-purpose investment product, and it isn't marketed as one.

01

Marketplace payment

PKC Marketplace lists goods from participating sellers. PKC Coin is one accepted payment method, settled on-chain.

02

Protocol staking

Holders may lock PKC in the staking contract for a chosen term. Rewards are paid from protocol parameters and are variable — not fixed or guaranteed.

03

On-chain settlement

Transactions clear on Binance Smart Chain, so anyone can verify balances, transfers, and contract activity independently.

Mechanics

How staking actually works

No step here promises a return. Rewards depend on the pool's parameters at the time you stake, and can change.

i.

Acquire PKC

Swap BNB for PKC on PancakeSwap and hold it in a self-custody wallet you control.

ii.

Lock in the staking contract

Connect your wallet, choose a lock period, and confirm the transaction. Your PKC remains locked until the term ends.

iii.

Claim rewards at term end

Rewards accrue per the contract's published rate and are claimable once the lock period completes. Rates are not fixed for life and may be adjusted for future stakes.

Before you act

Risk & legal disclosure

Written plainly, on purpose.

This is not financial advice

Nothing on this site is an offer, solicitation, or recommendation to buy or hold PKC Coin. Nothing here constitutes investment, legal, or tax advice. Evaluate any decision independently or with a licensed advisor.

Key risks

  • PKC Coin's price is volatile and can fall to zero. Staking rewards are variable, not guaranteed, and depend on protocol parameters that may change.
  • The smart contract has not yet completed an independent third-party audit. Unaudited code carries a higher risk of bugs or exploits.
  • Liquidity is not yet locked, which means it is technically possible for liquidity to be withdrawn from the trading pool.
  • The team currently operates under private, escrow-based identity verification rather than public disclosure. Treat this as a materially higher-risk configuration than a fully doxxed team.
  • Regulatory treatment of cryptoassets varies by jurisdiction and may change. It is your responsibility to confirm that acquiring or holding PKC Coin is lawful where you live.

No guaranteed returns

Any historical or displayed reward rate is not a promise of future performance. Do not stake or hold PKC Coin with funds you cannot afford to lose.
